Activity 2

Explaining Living Things

In this section, we will begin to formalise our understandings of living things and follow on from the previous activity to learn the fundamental features of living things. We will play some games to solidify our knowledge of these living things and then begin to compare two classes of living things: Plants and Animals. (ST1-4LW-S: Students are presented with an explanation of the external features of living things.)
