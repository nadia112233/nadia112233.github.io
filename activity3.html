Activity 3

Concept Map

When you click the link below you will be taken to a page which provides you with a half completed concept map. This concept map summarises the knowledge you have built so far on living and non-living things. Drag the green labels to their correct places to complete the mindmap. Think about your answers carefully! Once you are done, come back here and click on the button to check your answer. (ST1-2DP-T: Students apply higher order thinking skills to design a mindmap summarising their knowledge)

Click here to access the concept map activity.

Scenario Based Quiz

The below quiz will assess your understanding of living things further. You are given five scenarios with each one describing the features of either a living or non-living thing without naming it. Your job is to figure out whether the thing described in the scenario is living or non-living. Have a go below! (ST1-1WS-S: Students engage in a high order process of evaluating the evidence provided to come at a scientific conclusion)
