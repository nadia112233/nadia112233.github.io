Activity 1

Exploring Features!

In this section, we will explore some of the features of living things. Below there is a collection of six cards with pictures of both living and non-living things. First think about whether the thing in the picture is living or non-living. Flip the cards over by putting your cursor on them to reveal the features of the thing in the picture. Try to think about the common features that living things have and then the common features that non-living things have. Once you are done with all the cards, scroll down and answer the questions below. (ST1-4LW-S & ST1-1WS-S) Students have an opportunity to engage in a process of inquiry to determine the external features of living things.

The Sun

The sun can move in the sky. It emits light. It does not need any food or drink. It also doesn't grow even if it may appear like it does! Is it living or non-living?

Tree

Trees can grow and their growth also helps their branches move to absorb more sunlight. They need water and nutrients from the soil. You can also harvest seeds from the fruit of trees and plant them to get more trees! Are trees living or non-living?

Cat

Cats can move around everywhere! They need food and water to survive. They also breathe air. They can also have babies and those babies can grow up to be big cats. Is it living or non-living?

Car

Cars can move but they do not grow or change over time. They need fuel to run but if you don't use the car it won't need or use fuel. Cars do not breathe air. They also can not have babies. Cars can also make sounds just like cats! Is it living or non-living?

Water

Water can move but only if you touch it. It definitely can't grow! Water can also make sounds like splashing. Is it living or non-living?

Ant

Ants can move and grow. They spend all day collecting food and they need to drink water to live. Ants can also have babies. Ants can also make sounds but they are so quiet you can never hear them! Is it living or non-living?

So what are the unique features of all living things?

Check any box which you think is a unique feature of a living thing.

All living things are able to move on their own. Only non-living things like water need your help to move.
Plants are definitely living things! They need food, water and air to survive. They can also move and grow, its just that they move and grow so slowly that you do not notice. They can also reproduce
Cars might be able to move around and make sounds but there are some things they can't do that living things can do. For example, cars can't grow at all. Cars also can't have babies.
Being able to make sound is not an important feature of a living thing. Some living things like plants can't make sounds at all.

The correct boxes are the main features of a living thing! You can use these four conditions as a checklist to decide if something is a living thing or not. Take some time to understand and think about what you have learnt and then click the button below to move on to the next activity.
