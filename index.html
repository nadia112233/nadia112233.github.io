Living Things!

Welcome to the Living Things digital learning resource!

By going through the activities in this website, you will learn more about what living things are and what features separate them from non-living things. First you will explore your own ideas about what a living thing is and have a go at classifying some things as living or non-living. Then you will learn about the unique features of living things and play lots of fun games to test your knowledge.

By completing this learning resource, you will learn:

  • The external features of lots of living things
  • How to identify plants and animals using thier external features

Welcome Teachers,

This learning resource contains activities to develop student understanding of living things in accordance with the Stage 1 Living World outcome ST1-4LW-S as the Knowledge and Understanding Outcome. Each activity also endeavours to meet Working Scientifically and Design and Production outcomes. You may find more detailed information about the syllabus outcomes focused on in the "Syllabus" page linked in the navigation bar at the top. The navigation bar will allow you to quickly check each activity as needed instead of working sequentially through the activities as students would. All text in blue provides information on syllabus outcomes met, teaching pedagogies, 5 E's adherence, Alternative Conceptions, relevant teacher information and occasionally citations to academic sources.
